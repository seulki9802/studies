Author Map


  • Red point = The author's place of birth
  • Black point = The author's place of death


  • Red Circle = Density of birth place
  • Black Circle = Density of death place


  • You can zoom and click with the mouse.
  • When you click on the point, you can see the author's information.
  • When you click on the circle, you can see the city name.
